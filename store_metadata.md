## Description

### English
Photo ID is the fastest way to create standardized ID photos from your smartphone.

**Features:**
- Create passport, visa, and ID photos for 50+ countries
- AI-powered face detection and alignment
- Automatic background removal and color change
- Photo quality validation
- Export JPEG/PNG with print-ready layouts
- 100% offline processing - your photos never leave your device

**Supported Documents:**
- Passport (all countries)
- Visa (USA, UK, Schengen, Japan, Korea, etc.)
- National ID (CCCD, My Number, etc.)
- Driver's License
- Student ID

**How it works:**
1. Select your country and document type
2. Take a selfie with our guided overlay
3. AI automatically detects and aligns your face
4. Choose background color (white, blue, red)
5. Export in print-ready format

**Privacy First:**
- 100% offline processing
- No photos uploaded to any server
- EXIF data stripped on export
- Your photos stay on your device
